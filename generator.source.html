Step 5

Import it on your Mac

  1. Open System Settings, then go to Keyboard.
  2. Open Text Replacements.
  3. Drag in Text Substitutions.plist.

You need a Mac for this import step. After you add it there, Apple can sync the text replacements to your iPhone and iPad with iCloud Drive.
